Biography

Warm Greetings! I'm currently a second year Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computer and Science of the Tsinghua University. I'm working on ITML projects under the guidance of Prof. Shu-Tao Xia. I completed my bachelor's degree with honor in Electronic Engineering (2010-2014) from Xidian University. My main research interests include machine learning, compressed sensing, image processing and computer vision, including image restoration and image quality assessmenet.

I am a visiting student (Oct. 2017 ~ Apr. 2018) in Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Lei Zhang.
